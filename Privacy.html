Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to SmartSite Labs. This policy outlines how we handle your data.

This Privacy Policy was last updated on October 4, 2025.

1. Information We Collect

We collect two main types of information: **Personal Information** and **Non-Personal Information**.

1.1 Personal Information (Directly Provided)

This includes data you voluntarily submit via contact forms, consultation requests, or email. Examples include your name, email address, phone number, and company name.

1.2 Non-Personal Information (Automatically Collected)

When you visit our site, we automatically collect data such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, and pages viewed, using tools like cookies and Google Analytics. This helps us understand visitor behavior and improve our service.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To **respond to inquiries** and provide requested services.
  • To **improve our website** functionality and user experience.
  • For **marketing and communication**, only if you have opted in.
  • To **comply with legal obligations**.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your Personal Information to others. We may share data with trusted third-party service providers (e.g., hosting partners, payment processors) only to the extent necessary to provide our services, and under strict confidentiality agreements.

4. Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information, including SSL encryption and secure server environments. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@smartsitelabs.com.
